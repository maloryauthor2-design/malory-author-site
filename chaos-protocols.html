About the Book

Rivers is an ex-SAS operator who doesn’t belong in this galaxy. He arrived without a map, without a manual, and without the LitRPG System that everyone else seems to have grown up inside. He also arrived with an elf pilot, a broken ship, and a working knowledge of how to break things faster than the System can patch them.

Chaos Protocols is the start of a space-western LitRPG that treats progression as a heist tool, treats authority as the antagonist, and treats character chemistry as the engine. Co-authored with Troy Osgood.

Published by Aethon Books. Audiobook narrated by Daniel Wisniewski.

For Readers Who…

love Firefly, The Expanse, or any story where a tight crew on a worse-than-they-deserved ship runs a system they can’t quite outrun. Banter-heavy, gun-heavy, and faster than it has any right to be.
