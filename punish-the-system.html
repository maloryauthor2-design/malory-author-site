About the Book

Connor Keene was alive, and then he wasn’t, and then he was something the System calls a Punisher. The world that resurrected him has rules, and most of them are designed to keep people like Connor in line. His backup is an insane System Sprite and a partner whose mind is being slowly co-tenanted by a murder-lizard. The establishment wants him to fall in line. He’s thinking, instead, about tearing the establishment down.

Punish the System is a gritty, fast, and unapologetic LitRPG about what happens when the people the System breaks decide to return the favour. Co-authored with Cassius Lange.
