About the Book

Journey to the Dark Tower picks up where Welcome to the Dark Ages left Morgan: alive, in a body that isn’t hers, in a century that doesn’t want her, with a ghost in her head who keeps grading her decisions. The progression system is more visible now. So is the cost.

The book carries the same engine that made Book 1 work — British dryness, cultivation mechanics that actually mean something, and a protagonist who’s allowed to be both clever and out of her depth. If you read Book 1, you know what you’re here for. If you didn’t, start there.

Audiobook performed by Jess Nahikian.
