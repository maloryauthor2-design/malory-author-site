About the Book

Psyker Marine: Book 2 moves James Thorne’s war from first contact into sustained combat. The squad dynamics that opened in Book 1 take real weight here — people who started as peers don’t end the book that way. The Aetherion’s offer is starting to look like a contract with terms nobody read.

Same tactical discipline as Book 1, with the System opening up and the engagements getting denser. The combat-LitRPG mechanics aren’t decoration — they’re the grammar the book reasons through.

Produced by Royal Guard. Audiobook in the omnibus performed by Matthew Michael Lewis and Renee Nolen.
