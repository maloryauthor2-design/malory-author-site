## Design / legal note
Format homage, **not** Spotify's trademark — no Spotify logo, wordmark, or brand colours, and no Spotify/Wrapped-branded audio. The violet duotone is the M&M anchor (#5B3A7A) brightened, so the native-artifact frame still threads to the series while reading as a year-in-review card. Same principle as the receipt/AITA/iOS frames: the main artifact looks like the real thing, the on-brand title card closes it.
