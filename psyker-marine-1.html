About the Book

James Thorne was a nobody — the kind of background-character recruit the military machine processes by the thousand. Then the aliens arrived, and the rules changed for everyone. The ones that changed for Thorne came with a System interface, a weaponised mind, and a body that responds to combat the way other people’s respond to caffeine.

What follows is a relentless, levelling-up war story in the tradition of hard combat sci-fi. Squad dynamics, alien hostiles, and a System that rewards exactly the kind of person James Thorne turns out to be when nobody’s looking.

Produced by Royal Guard. Omnibus audiobook performed by Matthew Michael Lewis and Renee Nolen.

For Readers Who…

love Galaxy’s Edge, Terran Fleet Command, or any progression fantasy that treats combat as a craft. Think Starship Troopers with a stat sheet — military sci-fi for readers who want the LitRPG machinery without losing the war-story spine.
