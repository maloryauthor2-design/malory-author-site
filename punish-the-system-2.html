About the Book

His Punisher Class has evolved, and every pull of his trigger now files a Summary Judgment that permanently deletes his enemies from the System — especially the Outsiders. Killing them hasn’t bought Earth a moment’s peace, though. It’s just put Connor Keene on a fast track to the world’s most wanted, and right on the High Houses’ agenda.

While the awakening System continues its experimental “improvements” to humanity, the real threat has escalated — from random dungeons and rampaging monsters to turning Earth into a desolate landscape stripped of all resources and life. The true architects of the apocalypse want their mission wrapped up double-quick, and they don’t much mind how genocidally that’s achieved.

Co-authored with Cassius Lange. Published by LitForge Press.
